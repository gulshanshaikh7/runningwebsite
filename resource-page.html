Training Tips

Route Planning

Analyze the routes around DC and Arlington, paying attention to terrain and traffic. Use apps to plan the most efficient path.

Hydration

Ensure proper hydration before and during the run. Identify water stations or carry your own supplies.

Safety Guidelines

Let someone know your route. Wear reflective gear when running early in the morning or evening.

Documentation

Keep your phone charged for photos. Take clear pictures of checkpoints. Make sure location services are enabled for verification.

Time Management

Start early to maximize daylight. Set realistic time goals for each checkpoint. Include buffer time for unexpected challenges.
